NCAA All-Sports Rosters 2025-26

515,085 college athletes · all 28 NCAA sports · 3 divisions · both genders · the House v. NCAA "year zero"

Hugging Face (DOI) Kaggle DOI: 10.57967/hf/9512

Individual-level roster data for all 28 NCAA championship and emerging sports, all three divisions (D1/D2/D3), both genders, athletic year 2025-26 — the first year under the House v. NCAA settlement (roster limits, revenue sharing, NIL). Covers ~91.8% of officially reported NCAA participations. De-identified (no names, no contact info); every field is an institution-published roster fact validated against the NCAA's official sponsor lists.
